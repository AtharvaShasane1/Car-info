Dodge

About Dodge

Dodge is an American automobile brand known for its powerful and performance-oriented vehicles. Founded in 1900 by Horace Elgin Dodge and John Francis Dodge, the company has a long history of producing cars, trucks, and SUVs that prioritize horsepower and aggressive styling. Dodge vehicles are renowned for their muscle car heritage, with models like the Challenger and Charger gaining iconic status. The brand continues to offer a thrilling driving experience combined with modern technology and bold design elements.

Dodge History

Dodge has a rich history in the automotive industry. From its early years as a parts supplier to becoming a major automobile manufacturer, Dodge has made significant contributions to the American car market. The brand gained popularity for its performance cars and introduced iconic models like the Charger and Challenger during the muscle car era. Dodge vehicles have evolved over time, incorporating modern technologies and features while staying true to their high-performance roots.
